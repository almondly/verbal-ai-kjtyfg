## How to Use

### Starting a Sentence

1. **Type or select the first word** (e.g., "I")
2. **Look at the suggestions** - connecting words like "am", "want", "need" appear first
3. **Select the word** that fits what you want to say
4. **Continue building** - the AI adapts to your sentence

### Building a Sentence

1. **Watch the suggestions change** as you add words
2. **Connecting words appear automatically** when grammatically appropriate
3. **Full sentence suggestions** appear for common phrases
4. **Select any suggestion** to add it to your sentence

### Using Categories

1. **Select a category** (e.g., "food", "actions", "feelings")
2. **Suggestions adapt** to show category-relevant words
3. **Connecting words still appear** to help build sentences
4. **Switch categories** anytime to access different vocabulary

## Troubleshooting

### "I don't see connecting words"

**Solution**: 
- Make sure you've typed at least one word
- Check that the word you typed needs a connecting word (e.g., "I" needs "am", "want" needs "to")
- Try typing a different word to see if suggestions change

### "Suggestions don't fit my sentence"

**Solution**:
- Try selecting a **category** that matches your topic
- Type more words to give the AI more context
- Use the **keyboard mode** for more control

### "Web predictions aren't working"

**Solution**:
- Check your internet connection
- The app will still work with local AI predictions
- Web predictions are a bonus feature, not required
