
# Grammar Correction Examples

## How the AI Helps Fix Your Sentences

The AI now automatically detects grammatically incomplete sentences and suggests corrections. Here are examples of what it can fix:

## 1. Missing "am", "is", or "are"

### Pattern: Subject + Adjective
When you forget the linking verb between a subject and an adjective:

**You type:** "I good"
**AI suggests:** "I am good" ✓

**You type:** "He happy"
**AI suggests:** "He is happy" ✓

**You type:** "They tired"
**AI suggests:** "They are tired" ✓

**You type:** "She sad"
**AI suggests:** "She is sad" ✓

**You type:** "We ready"
**AI suggests:** "We are ready" ✓

## 2. Missing "to" Before Verbs

### Pattern: Want/Need + Verb
When you forget "to" before an action verb:

**You type:** "I want go outside"
**AI suggests:** "I want to go outside" ✓

**You type:** "I need eat"
**AI suggests:** "I need to eat" ✓

**You type:** "He wants play"
**AI suggests:** "He wants to play" ✓

**You type:** "She likes read"
**AI suggests:** "She likes to read" ✓

**You type:** "We need go home"
**AI suggests:** "We need to go home" ✓

## 3. Subject-Verb Agreement

### Pattern: Matching Verbs to Subjects
When the verb doesn't match the subject:

**You type:** "He want water"
**AI suggests:** "He wants water" ✓

**You type:** "She need help"
**AI suggests:** "She needs help" ✓

**You type:** "They wants food"
**AI suggests:** "They want food" ✓

**You type:** "It look good"
**AI suggests:** "It looks good" ✓

## 4. Missing Articles ("the" or "a")

### Pattern: Verb + Noun
When you forget articles before nouns:

**You type:** "I want ball"
**AI suggests:** "I want the ball" ✓

**You type:** "I see dog"
**AI suggests:** "I see a dog" ✓

**You type:** "I need bathroom"
**AI suggests:** "I need the bathroom" ✓

**You type:** "He has toy"
**AI suggests:** "He has a toy" ✓

## How to Use Grammar Corrections

### Visual Indicators:
1. **Green Background** - Grammar corrections have a light green background
2. **Green Border** - A green left border indicates a grammar fix
3. **"✓ Grammar Fix" Badge** - Look for this badge on suggestions
4. **High Confidence** - Grammar corrections show 85-95% confidence

### Where to Find Them:
- Grammar corrections appear at the **top** of the suggestion list
- They have **higher priority** than other suggestions
- They're shown in both **Communication** and **Keyboard** modes

### How to Apply:
1. **Tap the suggestion** to replace your current sentence with the corrected version
2. **Or tap the next word** to add just the missing word (like "am", "to", "the")

## Tips for Best Results

### 1. Type Naturally
- Don't worry about perfect grammar
- The AI will suggest corrections as you type

### 2. Look for Green Badges
- Green = Grammar correction
- Blue = Full sentence suggestion
- Orange = Polite ending

### 3. Use Suggestions Early
- Grammar corrections appear after 2-3 words
- Apply them early for better flow

### 4. Combine with Other Features
- Grammar corrections work with:
  - Category-specific words
  - Time-based suggestions
  - Personal learning patterns

## Confidence Levels

### What the percentages mean:
- **95%+** - Very confident grammar fix (e.g., "I" → "am")
- **90-94%** - Confident fix (e.g., "want" → "to")
- **85-89%** - Good fix (e.g., subject-verb agreement)

### When to trust suggestions:
- ✅ Always trust 90%+ suggestions
- ✅ Usually trust 85%+ suggestions
- ⚠️ Review suggestions below 85%

## Advanced Features

### Multiple Corrections:
If your sentence has multiple issues, the AI will suggest the most important fix first:

**You type:** "I want go bathroom"
**AI suggests:** 
1. "I want to go to the bathroom" ✓ (Full correction)
2. "to" (First missing word)

### Context-Aware:
The AI understands context:

**You type:** "I good" (after "How are you")
**AI suggests:** "I am good" ✓ (Knows you're answering a question)

### Learning from You:
The AI learns your patterns:
- If you often say "I want to go outside", it will prioritize that
- If you frequently use certain phrases, they'll appear more often
- Your personal style is preserved while grammar is corrected

## Troubleshooting

### If corrections don't appear:
1. Make sure you've typed at least 2 words
2. Check that the sentence is actually incomplete
3. Look for the green badge in suggestions
4. Try typing a bit more for context

### If corrections seem wrong:
1. Check the confidence percentage
2. Try a different suggestion
3. The AI learns from your choices
4. Report persistent issues for improvement
