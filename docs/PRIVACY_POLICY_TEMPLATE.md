## Introduction

COMpanion ("we", "our", or "us") operates the COMpanion mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Custom Communication Tiles**: Text and images you create for communication tiles

- **Communication Patterns**: Words and phrases you use to improve AI suggestions (stored locally)

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use the app (e.g., frequency of use, features accessed)
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, app version

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- **Providing Core Functionality**: Enabling communication features and AI-powered suggestions
- **Personalization**: Learning your communication patterns to provide better suggestions
- **App Improvement**: Understanding how the app is used to improve features
- **Technical Support**: Diagnosing and fixing technical issues

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device:
- Communication tiles and custom images
- User preferences and settings
- Communication history and patterns

### Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you choose to enable cloud sync:
- Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
- Data is stored securely using Supabase
- You can delete your cloud data at any time

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your communication data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Track you across other apps or websites

We may share data only in these circumstances:
- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly agree to share data
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect our legal rights
- **Service Providers**: With trusted service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., Supabase for cloud storage)
