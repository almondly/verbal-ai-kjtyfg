**Q: Grammar suggestions not appearing?**
- Check that you've typed 2-6 words
- Ensure the sentence has a clear grammatical issue
- Try clearing the app cache

**Q: Wrong grammar suggestion?**
- The AI learns from your usage patterns
- You can ignore suggestions and type your intended sentence
- Report persistent issues for improvement

**Q: Too many grammar suggestions?**
- Grammar suggestions only appear for high-confidence corrections
- They won't interfere with intentional short phrases
- You can always ignore them and continue typing
