
# User Guide: AI Features

## Overview
The communication app now includes powerful AI features that learn from your usage and help you communicate faster and more effectively.

## Key Features

### 1. Smart Word Suggestions
As you build sentences, the app suggests relevant words based on:
- **Your History**: Words and phrases you use frequently
- **Context**: Related words based on the current topic
- **Time of Day**: Common phrases for morning, afternoon, evening
- **Preferences**: Your favorite colors, foods, activities

**How to Use**:
- Start building a sentence by tapping tiles
- Look at the suggestion row below the phrase bar
- Tap any suggestion to add it to your sentence

### 2. Full Sentence Predictions

Easily change verbs between present, past, and future tense!

**How to Use**:

### 4. Topic-Based Learning
The app learns which topics you talk about most and suggests related words.

**Topics Tracked**:
- School (class, teacher, homework, book)
- Food (eat, drink, hungry, lunch)
- Family (mum, dad, brother, sister)
- Play (game, toy, outside, park)
- Feelings (happy, sad, excited, tired)
- Home (house, bed, room, sleep)
- Time (morning, afternoon, now, later)

**How It Works**:
- When you talk about school, you'll see more school-related suggestions
- When you talk about food, you'll see more food-related suggestions
- The app remembers your favorite topics

### 5. Visual Enhancements
Each tile now shows:
- **Pictogram Icon**: Simple visual representation
- **Unsplash Photo**: Real photo matching the word (when available)
- **Colored Background**: Category-based colors for easy recognition

**Image Priority**:
1. Custom image (if you added one)
2. Unsplash photo (automatically fetched)
3. Pictogram icon (fallback)

### 6. Confidence Indicators
Suggestions show confidence levels:
- **Green Border**: High confidence (80%+) - Very likely to be useful
- **Orange Border**: Medium confidence (60-80%) - Probably useful
- **Red Border**: Lower confidence (<60%) - Might be useful

## Tips for Best Results

### 1. Use the App Regularly
- The more you use it, the smarter it gets
- It learns your communication patterns
- Suggestions improve over time

### 2. Complete Full Sentences
- When you speak a sentence, the app learns it
- Next time, it will suggest similar sentences
- This helps with faster communication

### 3. Explore Suggestions
- Try different suggestions to see what works
- The app learns which suggestions you prefer
- Unused suggestions get lower priority over time

### 4. Use Tense Switching
- Long press verbs to change tense
- This helps the app learn tense patterns
- Makes it easier to talk about past and future

### 5. Set Your Preferences
- Go to Settings â†’ AI Preferences
- Set your favorite color, food, activity, animal
- These will appear in suggestions more often

## Privacy & Data

### What's Stored
- Words and phrases you use
- Word combinations and patterns
- Time of day usage
- Topic preferences
- Tense usage

### What's NOT Stored
- Personal information
- Names (unless you add them as tiles)
- Locations
- Sensitive data

### Data Usage
- All data stays on your device and in your Supabase account
- Data is used only to improve YOUR suggestions
- No data is shared with other users
- You can reset learning data anytime in Settings

## Troubleshooting

### Suggestions Not Appearing
- Make sure you have an internet connection (for first-time setup)
- Try using the app more to build up learning data
- Check that AI features are enabled in Settings

### Wrong Suggestions
- The app learns over time - ignore wrong suggestions
- Use correct words/phrases more often
- Reset learning data in Settings if needed

### Images Not Loading
- Check internet connection
- Some words may not have Unsplash images
- Pictogram icons will show as fallback

### Tense Switching Not Working
- Make sure you're long-pressing (not tapping)
- Only works on verbs (action words)
- Some irregular verbs may not have all tenses
